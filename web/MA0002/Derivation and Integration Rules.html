Derivation

\(f(x)\) \(\frac{df}{dx}\)  
\(x^n \) \(nx^{n-1}\) \(n \neq 0\)
\(\mathrm{sin}(ax)\) \(a\;\mathrm{cos}(ax)\)  
\(\mathrm{cos}(ax)\) \(-a\;\mathrm{sin}(ax)\)  
\(\mathrm{tan}(ax)\) \(a\;\mathrm{sec}^2(ax) = \frac{a}{\mathrm{cos}^2(ax)} = a - a\;\mathrm{tan}^2(ax)\)  
\(e^x\) \(e^x\)  
\(a^x\) \((\mathrm{ln}\; a)a^x\)  
\(\mathrm{ln}\;x\) \(\frac{1}{x}\)  
\(\mathrm{ln}\;f(x)\) \(\frac{f'(x)}{f(x)}\)  
\(\mathrm{log}_a\,x\) \(\frac{1}{(\mathrm{ln}\; a)\,x}\)
