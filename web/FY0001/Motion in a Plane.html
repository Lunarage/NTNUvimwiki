Motion in a Plane

TL;DR

  • Motion of a projectile:
  • \(v_x = v_0x = v_0\mathrm{cos}\,\theta\)
  • \(v_y = v_{0_y} - gt = v_0\mathrm{sin}\,\theta - gt\)
  • \(x = v_{0_x}t\)
  • \(y = v_{0_y}t - \frac{1}{2}gt^2\)
  • Centripetal acceleration of uniform circular motion: \(a = \frac{a^2}{r}\)
