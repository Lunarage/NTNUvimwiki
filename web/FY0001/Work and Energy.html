Work and Energy

Work

\[ W = \vec{F} \cdot \vec{\Delta x} \]

Work as a Variable Force

Work done by a variable force

\[ W = \bar{\vec{F}} \cdot \vec{\Delta x} \]

Kinetic Energy

Kinetic energy

\[ K = \frac{1}{2}mv^2 \]

Work-energy theorem

\[ \Delta K = W \]

Potential Energy

Potential energy in a gravitational field

\[ U = mgh \]

Mechanical energy

\[ E = K + U \]

Conservation of mechanical energy

\[ E = K + U = [constant] \]

Potential Energy of a Conservative Force

Potential energy of a spring

\[ U = \int kx \,dx = \frac{1}{2}kx^2 \]

Other Forms of Energy

Power

Average power

\[ \bar{P} = \frac{W}{\Delta t} \]

Instantaneous power

\[ P = \lim_{\Delta t \to 0} \frac{W}{\Delta t} \]

Power delivered by a force

\[ P = \vec{F} \cdot \vec{v} \]
