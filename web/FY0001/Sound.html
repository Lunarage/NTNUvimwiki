Sound Waves In Air

Intensity of Sound

Intensity in dB

\[ \mathrm{dB} = (\mathrm{10\,dB}) \times \mathrm{log}_{10}\left(\frac{\mathrm{W \cdot m^{-2}}}{1\times10^{-12}\mathrm{W \cdot m^{ -2 }}}\right) \] \[ I \propto \frac{1}{r^2} \] \[ \frac{I_1}{I_2} = \frac{r_1^2}{r_2^2} \]

The Speed of Sound

\[ v \approx 331\,\mathrm{m \cdot s^{-1}} \]

The Doppler Effect

\[ f = f_0\left(\frac{v+v_r}{v+v_s}\right) \]

Where \(v\) is the speed of waves in the medium, \(f\) is the observed frequency, \(f_0\) is the emitted frequency, \(v_r\) is the velocity of the receiver relative to the medium and \(v_s\) is the speed of the source relative to the medium.
