Oscillations

TL;DR

  • Simple harmonic motion: \(x = A\cos{\omega t}\)
  • Period: \(T = \frac{2\pi}{\omega}\)
  • Frequency: \(f = \frac{1}{T} = \frac{\omega}{2\pi}\)
  • Equation of motion of simple harmonic oscillator: \(ma = -kx\)
  • Angular frequency of simple harmonic oscillator: \(\omega = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}\)
  • Period of simple harmonic oscillator: \(T = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{m}{k}}\)
  • Energy of simple harmonic oscillator: \(E = \frac{1}{2}kA^2\)
  • Angular frequency of simple pendulum: \(\omega = \sqrt{\frac{g}{l}}\)
  • Period of simple pendulum: \(\omega = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{l}{g}}\)

Simple Harmonic Motion

\[ x = A\,\mathrm{cos}(\omega t) \]

Where \(A\) is the amplitude and \(\omega\) is the angular frequency.

Period

\[ T = \frac{2\pi}{\omega} \]

Frequency

\[ f = \frac{1}{T} = \frac{\omega}{2\pi} \] \[ a = -\omega^2x \]

The Simple Harmonic Oscillator

Mass on a spring

\[ ma = -kx \] \[ \omega^2 = \frac{k}{m} \] \[ \omega = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}} \] \[ f = \frac{\omega}{2\pi} = \frac{1}{2\pi} \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}} \] \[ T = \frac{1}{f} = 2\pi \sqrt{\frac{m}{k}} \] \[ x = A\,\mathrm{cos}\left(\sqrt{\frac{m}{k}}t\right) \] \[ v = -\omega A\,\mathrm{sin}(\omega t) \]

Kinetic Energy and Potential Energy

\[ K = \frac{1}{2}mv^2 = \frac{1}{2}m[-\omega A\,\mathrm{sin}(\omega t)]^2 = \frac{1}{2}m\omega^2A^2\mathrm{sin}^2(\omega t) \] \[ m\omega^2 = k \] \[ K = \frac{1}{2}kA^2\mathrm{sin}^2(\omega t) \] \[ U = \frac{1}{2}kx^2 = \frac{1}{2}k[A\,\mathrm{cos}(\omega t)]^2 = \frac{1}{2}kA^2\mathrm{cos}^2(\omega t) \] \[ K + U = \frac{1}{2}kA^2\mathrm{sin}^2(\omega t) + \frac{1}{2}kA^2\mathrm{cos}^2(\omega t) = \frac{1}{2}kA^2(\mathrm{cos}^2(\omega t)+\mathrm{sin}^2(\omega t)) \]

Energy of a simple harmonic oscillator

\[ E = K + U = \frac{1}{2}kA^2 \]

The Simple Pendulum

\[ I\alpha = \tau \]

Where \(I\) is the moment of inertia and \(\tau\) is the torque.

\[ \tau = -mgl\,\mathrm{sin}\theta \]

Where \(l\) is the length of the pendulum.

\[ I = ml^2 \] \[ l\alpha = -g\,\mathrm{sin}\theta \] \[ \omega = \sqrt{\frac{g}{l}} \] \[ f = \frac{\omega}{2\pi} = \frac{1}{2\pi}\sqrt{\frac{g}{l}} \]
