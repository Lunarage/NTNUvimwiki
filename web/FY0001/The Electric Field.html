The Electric Field

TL;DR

Definition of electric field

\[ E = \frac{F}{q} \]

Electric field of point charge

\[ E = k_e\frac{q'}{r^2} \]

Electric field of large, uniform charged flat sheet

\[ E = \frac{\sigma}{2\epsilon_0} \]

Electric field of charged line

\[ E = \frac{1}{2}k_e\frac{\lambda}{r} \]

The Electric Field

\[ \sigma = \frac{Q}{A} \]

Where \(Q\) is the charge and \(A\) is the area.
