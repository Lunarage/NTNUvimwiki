Average Speed

\[ \bar{v} = \frac{d}{t} \]

Average Velocity for Motion Along a Straight Line

\[ \bar{v} = \frac{x_2 - x_1}{t_2 - t_1} \]

aka

\[ \bar{v} = \frac{\Delta x}{\Delta t} \]

Instantaneous Velocity

\[ v = \frac{\Delta x}{\Delta t} \text{ with extremely small } \Delta t \]

aka

\[ v = \lim_{\Delta t \to 0} \frac{\Delta x}{\Delta t} \]

Acceleration

Average Acceleration

\[ \bar{a} = \frac{v_2 - v_1}{t_2 - t_1} \]

aka

\[ \bar{a} = \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} \]

Instantaneous Acceleration

\[ v = \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} \text{ with extremely small } \Delta t \]

aka

\[ v = \lim_{\Delta t \to 0} \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} \]

Motion with Constant Acceleration

Velocity

\[ v = v_0 + at \]

Distance

\[ x = x_0 + v_0t + \frac{1}{2}at^2 \] \[ x - x_0 = v_0t + \frac{1}{2}at^2 \]

Timeless equation

\[ \begin{aligned} t & = \frac{v-v_0}{a} \\ x-x_0 & = v_0(\frac{v-v_0}{a}) + \frac{1}{2}a(\frac{v-v_0}{a})^2 \\ & = \frac{v_0v - v_0^2}{a} + \frac{\frac{1}{2}(v^2-2vv_0 + v_0^2)}{a} \\ & = \frac{\frac{1}{2}(v^2-v_0^2)}{a} \end{aligned} \] \[ a(x - x_0) = \frac{1}{2}(v^2 - v_0^2) \]

For velocity

\[ v^2 - v_0^2 = 2a(x - x_0) \]

For distance

\[ x - x_0 = \frac{v^2 - v_0^2}{2a} \]

The Acceleration of Free Fall

Average gravitational acceleration at the surface of the earth

\[ g \simeq 9.81 \mathrm{ms}^{-2} \]

Free fall (positive direction upwards)

\[ v = v_0 - gt \] \[ x = x_0 + v_0t - \frac{1}{2}gt^2 \] \[ -g(x - x_0) = \frac{1}{2}(v^2 - v_0^2) \]
