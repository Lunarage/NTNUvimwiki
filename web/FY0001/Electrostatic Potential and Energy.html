Electrostatic Potential and Energy

TL;DR

  • Definition of electrostatic potential: \(V = \frac{U}{q}\)
  • Potential energy of two point charges: \(U = k_e\frac{qq'}{r}\)
  • Electrostatic potential of point charge: \(V = k_e\frac{q'}{r}\)

The Electrostatic Potential

\[ V = U/q \]

where \(V\) is the electrostatic potential, \(U\) is the electric potential energy, and \(q\) is the electric charge.
